Little misteries in Caen

Discover the dark side of Caen! An unusual tour retracing the icy stories of haunted houses, urban legends and tragic events that took place in our beautiful downtown Caen.

Every Tuesday from 2pm - duration: 1h30 - 14 euros per person.

The Gourm'Handi'Ses workshop, its chocolates and biscuits

You may know the Gourm'handi'ses boutique located on rue de la fontaine in Caen. This visit will be an opportunity to discover how chocolates and biscuits are made by the Etablissement de Service et d'Aide par le Travail de Colombelles, what are the specificities of a solidarity company.

Every Thursday from 10am - duration: 45mn - 7 euros per person.

In the footsteps of Charlotte Corday

An itinerary in the city in the wake of Charlotte Corday, the opportunity to retrace the trajectory of what Lamartine called "the angel of murder" and to retrace some of the great revolutionary episodes that took place in Caen.

Every Thursday from 10am - duration: 2h30 - 7 euros per person.

Cooking demonstration at Riva-Bella-Thalazur restaurant

This meeting will be an opportunity to meet Chef Patrick Durant, who will share with you his love of cooking through the execution of a recipe of his secret. It will also be an opportunity to evoke the fundamentals of his cuisine, namely the "Five S's": Seasonal products full of Flavour, resulting from exceptional geographical situations, cooked in a healthy and simple way.

Every Monday from 8pm - duration: 1 hour - 45 euros per person.

The Vaugueux and the mountain of the Sepulchre

Located at the foot of four hills, the city of Caen lies in the lower valley of the Orne. The hills were called mountains or mounts very commonly in the Middle Ages. The suburb of Le Vaugueux, well hidden, is not ignored by tourists; it is in a way our place in Le Tertre.

From June to September, Wednesdays from 2pm - duration: 1 hour - 5 euros per person.
